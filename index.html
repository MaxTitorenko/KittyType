Info

About

KittyType is a web app to test typing speed and accuracy

Time selection

You have three options on the interface: 15sec, 30sec and 60sec.
You can choose any of them by clicking on the number.

Difficulty selection

You have three options on the interface: Easy, Medium and Hard.
Easy difficulty generates text without capital letters and punctuation.
Medium difficulty generates text with capital letters.
Hard difficulty generates text with capital letters and punctuation.
You can choose any difficulty clicking on difficulty name.

Hotkey

You can press F2 insted of the button under the text area to restart/generate new text.
